fomous.co

Famous.co is a beautiful website that gets the most out its design. The website have a really nice looking layout, which is a little pushed to the right since the navigation menu icon is on the left, accompanied by negative space below it. Their selection of videos and images matches and helps for the desired look and the feel of the website. I have to mention that the position of some of the images is a little odd sometimes but it looks good to the eye. They achieve this effect by placing a portion of the image off of the viewport.

Smooth transitions between each of the sections of the website also make it very pleasant to the eyes. Also not placing everything centered gives the website a different sense of balance than what most people is used to. The minimalism of design on this website really evokes a sense of cleanness and professionalism towards this company. I personally think this website is a piece of really good design.

Website developed by Miguel Cordero.
